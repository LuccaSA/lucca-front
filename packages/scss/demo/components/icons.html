You can add a mod to change your icon size:

heart <i class="icon mod-smaller">heart</i>
heart <i class="icon mod-small">heart</i>
heart <i class="icon">heart</i>
heart <i class="icon mod-big">heart</i>
heart <i class="icon mod-bigger">heart</i>
heart <i class="icon mod-biggest">heart</i>
Tip: You can manually set the icon size by giving it a font-size value.
