Form mod-framed

Form has can have a mod called .mod-framed. In this mod .form-group behave differently and you have access to a new DOM level called .form-group-line.

form-group represents a whole section of your form. It can have a .form-group-title to name your section It can contain .form-group-line and .input.mod-framed

In a form-group-line you can use .grid-* classes onto your .input to size your inputs on a single line.

<form class="form mod-framed"> <div class="form-group"> <h2 class="form-group-title">Title name</h2> <div class="form-group-line"> <div class="input mod-framed grid-xs8> [...] </div> <div class="input mod-framed grid-xs4> [...] </div> </div> </div> </form>
