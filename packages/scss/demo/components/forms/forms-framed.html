Framed form

Form can have a mod called mod-framed. In this mod form-group behave differently and you have access to a new DOM level called form-group-line.

You can divide form-group-line using form-group-line-* classes (like form-group-line-xs6 or form-group-line-lg4) over your inputs to size them on a single line.
