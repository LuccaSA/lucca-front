Auto width

You can remove the columns numbers to devide the grid uniformly:

grid-s
grid-s
grid-s
grid-s
grid-s
<div class="grid"> <div class="grid-s">...</div> <div class="grid-s">...</div> ... </div> Tip: It will also work with -xs, -sm, -md, -lg, -xl, -xxl, or -lg

Horizontal alignment

You can align columns horizontaly inside the grid:

<div class="grid mod-xsStart"> ... <div class="grid mod-xsCenter"> ... <div class="grid mod-xsEnd"> ... Tip: It will also work with -xs, -sm, -md, -lg, -xl, -xxl, or -lg

Vertical alignment

You can align columns verticaly inside the grid:

<div class="grid mod-xsTop"> ... <div class="grid mod-xsMiddle"> ... <div class="grid mod-xsBottom"> ... Tip: It will also work with -xs, -sm, -md, -lg, -xl, -xxl, or -lg
