Horizontal alignment

You can align the content horizontaly inside the column:

<div class="grid mod-start@mediaMinXXXS"> ... <div class="grid mod-center@mediaMinXXXS"> ... <div class="grid mod-end@mediaMinXXXS"> ... Tip: It will also work with XXS, XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL and XXXL

Vertical alignment

You can align columns verticaly inside the grid:

<div class="grid mod-top@mediaMinXXXS"> ... <div class="grid mod-middle@mediaMinXXXS"> ... <div class="grid mod-bottom@mediaMinXXXS"> ... Tip: It will also work with XXS, XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL and XXXL
