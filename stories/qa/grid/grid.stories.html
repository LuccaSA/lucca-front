Horizontal alignment

You can align the content horizontaly inside the column:

<div class="grid-column" style="--grid-justify: start">…</div>> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-justify: center">…</div>> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-justify: end">…</div>>

Vertical alignment

You can align columns verticaly inside the grid:

<div class="grid-column" style="--grid-align: start">…</div>> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-align: center">…</div>> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-align: end">…</div>>

Reorder

You can reorder columns with utilities:

1 --grid-column: 2
2 --grid-column: 3
3 --grid-column: 1
4
1
2 --grid-columnAtMediaMinM: 4
3

<div class="grid"> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-column: 2">…</div> <div class="grid-column" style="--grid-column: 1">…</div> </div> Tip: It will also work with suffix AtMediaMin.
